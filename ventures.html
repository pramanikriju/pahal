What is National Policy Summit?

The team of Pahal : Niti Shastra is organizing the first ever Policy Summit in the state of Bihar, in the second week of June, 2018 with committees like Bihar Vikas Mission, Gram Sabha, All India Congress Committee, and State Round Table Conference on Education, Bal Sabha being simulated.

The event will be a pristine summit, offering unprecedented experience in the field of policy making to the youth of Bihar and elsewhere.

There shall also be a delegation of about 50 underprivileged students from government schools and a platform for Senior Citizens’ Dialogue as a part of our responsibility towards the society. And of the funds received as sponsorships and fees, the remainder will be donated to various relief funds as a part of national service. We shall be summiting the reports of each committee to the concerned government organizations.
