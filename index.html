From the Founder's Desk

"Be the Change you want to see" - Mahatma Gandhi

For me, education has never been a business, rather it is the most beautiful community service one can ever provide. Seeing a change is what everyone desires but waiving the change takes something called courage. Pahal is nothing but a initiative of change. Change to mark a difference. Change to bring 'all under one'. Pahal is something which wont allow me a peaceful sleep until it happens right. Right in the sense of drawing out a change.

Mates, I proudly present you my dream project "Pahal: Niti Shastra" a platform which will provide you opportunities to serve the society in the least way you can, a platform which will give you a chance to meet the specialty privileged sections of society and a platform which will help me to bring 'all under one'.Come be a part Pahal family. Experience the upcoming change in the society along with the present and future of nation, while we will serve you with best of business commitments and professionalism you aspire for.
